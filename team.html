Project Goals

Our primary objectives include:

  • Ensuring user privacy and security with customizable content visibility and strict access controls.
  • Providing a user-friendly platform for families to securely share moments, with privacy options for each post.
  • Building a scalable platform that grows with user needs while maintaining a high level of security and performance.
  • Offering features that build trust within the community, such as an authenticity rating system and temporary post functionality.
  • Delivering an intuitive and visually appealing user experience on both mobile and desktop platforms.
  • Encouraging a sense of community and connection while maintaining the highest standards of privacy and content authenticity.
