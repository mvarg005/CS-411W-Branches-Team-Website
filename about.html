About

We are a dedicated team working towards solving real-world problems with innovative software solutions.

Project Goals

Our primary objectives include:

  • Developing an innovative solution to address privacy concerns in family sharing platforms.
  • Ensuring scalability and adaptability for families of different sizes and locations.
  • Creating a user-friendly interface that simplifies the process of sharing content securely.
  • Meeting project timelines while maintaining the highest standards of security and privacy.
Problem Statement

Families struggle to securely share important updates online due to inadequate privacy settings on traditional platforms.

Problem Solution

Create a secure platform for families to share memories with advanced privacy controls and customizable settings.

Software Solution: Branches

Branches is a family-focused social media platform offering secure sharing, temporary posts, and private connections.
